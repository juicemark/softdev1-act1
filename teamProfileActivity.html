FAQ

What is this website about?

- The project aims to create a group profile featuring trivial information about each member, including pictures and a group photo with a caption. We used nested tables to organize and present this information effectively.

What year and class is this project for?

- This project is part of the curriculum for Computer Engineering students in the 3rd year, first semester. It is designed to provide practical experience in developing HTML code using nested tables to showcase group information.

What challenges did you face during the project?

- One of the main challenges we encountered was obtaining our group photo. Unfortunately, some of our members fell ill during the project timeline, making it difficult to schedule and capture the group photo as planned.

What did we learn from this project?

- Through this project, we gained valuable experience in collaborating effectively to develop HTML code, namely enhancing our teamwork skills by working together towards a common goal and gaining insights into the importance of communication and flexibility in project execution.

What kind of projects does the team work on?

- The team works on various projects ranging from web development to data analysis. More details can be found in the individual team member profiles.

Where is your group located? How can we contact you?

- Our group is located on Holy Angel University. We can be contacted through our individual profiles, either on our email or on our contact numbers.
